<p>The second rumored subject of the song is <a data-api_path="/artists/58994" href="https://genius.com/artists/Boys-like-girls" rel="noopener">Boys Like Girls</a> front-man, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Martin_Johnson_%28musician" rel="noopener nofollow">Martin Johnson</a> whom <a href="https://www.complex.com/music/2012/10/a-history-of-people-taylor-swift-has-written-songs-about/martin-johnson" rel="noopener nofollow">Swift had a brief fling with in 2008</a> and collaborated with to pen the band’s track <a data-api_path="/songs/642957" href="https://genius.com/Boys-like-girls-two-is-better-than-one-lyrics" rel="noopener">“Two is Better Than One”</a> and Taylor’s song <a data-api_path="/songs/186961" href="https://genius.com/Taylor-swift-if-this-was-a-movie-lyrics" rel="noopener">“If This Was a Movie”</a>. <p>The lyrics talk about disapproval against the relationship, and her desire to go somewhere far away with him, and escape the people who look down on them.</p>
<p>“Love Story” peaked at #1 on <em>Billboard</em>’s Hot Country Songs, and at #5 on the <em>Billboard</em> Hot 100, just weeks after its release.</p>
